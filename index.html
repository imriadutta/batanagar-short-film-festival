About Us

The Batanagar Short Film Festival, conceived by the visionary Mohanlal Mukherjee, made its remarkable debut in 2019. Born from the passion and dedication of film enthusiasts in Batanagar, this festival has quickly become a celebrated platform for aspiring filmmakers, artists, and cinema lovers alike.

A Cinematic Journey

At its inception, the Batanagar Short Film Festival was a dream shared by a group of film lovers determined to foster a love for good cinema. Guided by the belief that films possess the power to evoke emotions, provoke thought, and inspire change, the festival set out on a mission to curate a diverse selection of short films that encapsulate the essence of storytelling in its purest form.

Embracing Culture and Creativity

More than just a celebration of films, the Batanagar Short Film Festival endeavors to embrace the richness of culture and creativity. Alongside the captivating cinematic experience, the festival's attendees are treated to a delightful fusion of music, dance, theater, and other artistic expressions that further enrich their cultural journey.

Unveiling New Horizons

As the festival gained momentum and popularity, its vision expanded to encompass a broader array of creative ventures. The Batanagar Short Film Festival now aspires to not only showcase short films but also to inspire the next generation of storytellers and filmmakers. In the near future, we aim to introduce scriptwriting and film making workshops, providing aspiring artists with the tools they need to bring their stories to life on the silver screen.

Educational Empowerment

Understanding the potential of cinema as a powerful educational tool, the Batanagar Short Film Festival is committed to conducting film making workshops in educational institutions. By nurturing young minds and fostering creativity, we hope to instill the art of visual storytelling in the hearts of the next generation.

A Journey from Unity to Association

The Batanagar Short Film Festival had its roots in the esteemed guidance of the 'Batanagar Theatre Unity.' Over time, the festival's growth and accomplishments paved the way for its independent existence, now proudly organized by the 'Batanagar Parthivan Cultural Association.' This transition has only strengthened our resolve to continue the legacy of promoting good films and nurturing creative expressions.

Join Us in the Celebration of Art and Vision

As we embark on yet another exhilarating cinematic journey, we extend a warm invitation to all film enthusiasts, aspiring artists, and culture aficionados to be a part of the Batanagar Short Film Festival. Come, witness the magic of storytelling unfold through captivating short films, immerse yourself in the tapestry of culture, and embrace the boundless creativity that surrounds us.
